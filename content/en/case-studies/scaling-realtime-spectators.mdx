<Callout type="problem">
UFT runs on a single Dockerized VPS today, sized for the traffic it currently sees (~1,700 tracked users). A spectator-facing product is inherently spikier than an admin dashboard — usage concentrates around live matches — and that's a different load shape than what the current infrastructure has been tested against.
</Callout>
