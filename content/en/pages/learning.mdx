UFT isn't finished — I keep evolving it, and I try to document the reasoning behind each meaningful change rather than just shipping it. The [Case Studies](/case-studies) section is the first version of that habit: a written record of a problem, what I considered, and what I chose, close enough to when I made the decision that I still remember why.

The next step is turning that habit into something more structured: a public, ongoing log of engineering decisions across UFT and future projects — **Engineering Academy**.
