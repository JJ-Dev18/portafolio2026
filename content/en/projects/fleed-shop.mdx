## Overview

**Fleed Shop** was an e-commerce/shop module developed for a UK company. The goal was to deliver a solid foundation to sell products and manage subscription packages with a modern purchase flow, an administration panel, and integrated payments.

The project reached a functional implementation, but it **did not continue** due to disagreements around long-term maintenance and support costs.

---

## What it includes

The shop includes a **product catalog**, cart and checkout, plus a **subscription** flow based on packages. The UI was built with **Material UI** and **Tailwind CSS**, including animations via Framer Motion and reusable components organized by domain (shopping cart, subscriptions, and administration).

On the admin side, protected views and API routes were implemented to manage key entities (products, packages, sales, and subscriptions), restricting access to **ADMIN** users only.

---

## Auth, roles, and i18n

Authentication was implemented with **NextAuth**, supporting Google OAuth and credentials (email/password). The system uses roles such as **USER** and **ADMIN** to protect the admin panel and sensitive routes.

Internationalization was also added through **next-i18next** (es/en), keeping the UI consistent across languages.

---

## Data and persistence

The database runs on **PostgreSQL** (Dockerized for development). **Prisma** was used for modeling core entities, while **Knex** handled migrations and seeds to bootstrap environments and test data reliably.

---

## Payments and subscriptions (Stripe)

The module integrates **Stripe** for checkout and subscriptions. Packages are mapped to Stripe products/prices, leveraging metadata for business rules (including specific constraints for a custom package).
