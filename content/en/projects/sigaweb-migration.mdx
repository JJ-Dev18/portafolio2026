## 🛠️ The Technical Challenge
The main challenge was managing the infrastructure for the domains `sigaweb.co` and `sigaweb.com.co`. The system ran on **CentOS** servers with manual configurations, which made scaling difficult and increased operational costs.

## 🏗️ Architecture Solution
I implemented an **infrastructure modernization** strategy:
- **Dockerization:** Packaging the PHP/Laravel application and dependent services in containers to ensure environment parity.
- **Reverse Proxy:** **Nginx** configuration to manage traffic from multiple domains and SSL certificates.
- **OS Migration:** Transition from CentOS to lighter, easier-to-maintain container-based environments.
- **Database:** Optimization of **MySQL** instances to improve platform response times.

## 📈 Results and Impact
- **Cost Reduction:** By dockerizing services, we achieved better server resource utilization, reducing monthly infrastructure costs.
- **Safe Deployments:** Reduction of production errors thanks to container standardization.
- **Continuous Support:** Proactive service maintenance to ensure high availability of the platform for Sigaweb SAS clients.

> "Moving from manual configurations on physical servers to a container architecture allowed us to gain agility and drastically reduce failure points."
