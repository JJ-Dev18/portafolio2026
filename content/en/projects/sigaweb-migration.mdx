## 🛠️ The Technical Challenge
The main challenge was stabilizing and modernizing the infrastructure for `sigaweb.co` and `sigaweb.com.co`. The system ran on **CentOS** with manual, tightly-coupled configurations, which made scaling harder, increased operational risk, and raised maintenance costs.

## 🏗️ Architecture Solution
I executed an **infrastructure modernization** plan focused on standardization and reliability. The PHP/Laravel application and its dependencies were dockerized to ensure environment parity and repeatable deployments. At the edge, **Nginx** was configured as a reverse proxy to handle multi-domain traffic and SSL certificates in a centralized, maintainable way.

As part of the transition away from “manual server setups”, operations moved toward lighter, easier-to-maintain container-based environments, and **MySQL** instances were tuned to improve performance and stability under load.

## 📈 Results and Impact
The shift to dockerized services improved server resource utilization and delivered **cost reductions** in monthly infrastructure spend. Container standardization reduced production errors and enabled safer, more predictable deployments.

In addition, proactive maintenance routines were introduced to sustain high availability and reduce incident frequency for Sigaweb SAS clients.

> "Moving from manual configurations on physical servers to a container architecture allowed us to gain agility and drastically reduce failure points."
