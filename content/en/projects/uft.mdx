## Overview

**UFT** is a full-stack platform built to **manage, score, and track Ultimate Frisbee tournaments in real time**. It unifies tournament administration, live scorekeeping, and player/team statistics into a single experience for organizers and spectators.

In production, UFT supports tournaments with multiple concurrent games while keeping data consistent and visible instantly across dashboards.

---

## The Problem

Many tournaments still depend on manual scorekeeping and fragmented tools (spreadsheets, WhatsApp, PDFs). That usually results in delayed stats, inconsistent data, and low traceability: numbers arrive late, get duplicated, or get lost, and fans don’t get a compelling live experience.

The outcome is **poor data quality**, **slow reporting**, and **limited fan engagement**, plus unnecessary operational friction for organizers.

---

## The Solution

UFT centralizes tournament operations with **real-time score tracking**, **live player and team statistics**, and a dedicated **management dashboard**. It also includes a roadmap for spectator-focused experiences (mobile app in progress) and event/media coverage features aimed at improving engagement.

---

## Technical Architecture

### Frontend
The admin and public dashboards are built with **React 18**. The spectator app is being developed with **React Native + TypeScript**. UI consistency and iteration speed come from Tailwind CSS and shadcn/ui.

### Backend
The backend is implemented with **NestJS**, exposing a RESTful API and using **PostgreSQL** for reliable persistence and consistency under concurrency.

### Infrastructure
The platform runs as **dockerized services**, behind an **Nginx** reverse proxy, deployed on a VPS (DigitalOcean). SSL, environment isolation, and a mix of manual and automated backups were set up to support safe production operations.
